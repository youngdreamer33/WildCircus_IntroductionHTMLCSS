About Us

More than a Circus, a Clique with a Purpose

We are a group of people with different backgrounds who share the same dreams and purpose. Our philosophy is very simple: come as you are. We learnt it from our former employer but we work really hard to make it ours too. We want our guests to have the best experience possible. We want them to be part of the show, live it to the fullest and not only watch it.

At the Wild Circus Company, we value humane health and happiness as much as we care about our animals. No animal is hurt during our shows or between. Some of our competitors are not like that: they are not wild, they are simply inhumane. Please don't support their actions, don't be a part of those atrocities.

We can make this world better, together: that's our purpose. This Circus is just a mere drop in the Ocean but without mere drops they wouldn’t be any Ocean…
